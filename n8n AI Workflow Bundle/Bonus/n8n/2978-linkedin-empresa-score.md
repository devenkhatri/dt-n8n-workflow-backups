# Workflow Analysis for LinkedIn Company Score Calculator

## Description
This workflow calculates a score for LinkedIn companies based on various engagement metrics, company attributes, and job postings, providing insights into their activity and relevance.

## Input Details
This workflow is triggered manually and requires company data as input.

## Process Summary
The workflow begins by manually receiving company data. It then processes the company's LinkedIn profile URL to extract the profile ID. Subsequent steps involve fetching employee details, retrieving company details, and analyzing job postings to gather key metrics. Finally, a score is calculated based on these aggregated metrics.

## Output Details
The workflow outputs a comprehensive company score.
