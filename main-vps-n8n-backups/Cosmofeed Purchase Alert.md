# Workflow Analysis for Cosmofeed Purchase Alert

## Description
This workflow sends purchase alerts, likely notifying relevant parties when a purchase is made through Cosmofeed.

## Input Details
The workflow is triggered by a purchase event from Cosmofeed, receiving purchase-related data.

## Process Summary
The workflow receives purchase data from Cosmofeed. It processes this data to extract relevant purchase details. An alert notification is then generated based on the purchase information. The alert is sent to configured recipients or systems.

## Output Details
The workflow produces and sends a purchase alert notification to designated recipients or systems.
