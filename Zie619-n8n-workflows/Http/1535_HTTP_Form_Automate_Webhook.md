## Process Summary
First, the workflow uses Perplexity to research the provided topic. Next, an AI agent (GPT-4) generates an SEO-optimized blog article based on the research. The AI agent then publishes this article to WordPress. Subsequently, it sends an email notification to a specified address and a Slack notification to a designated channel with the article details. Finally, the workflow logs the article's information into a Notion database.

## Output Details
The workflow produces an SEO-optimized blog article published on WordPress, sends email and Slack notifications, and records article details in a Notion database.
